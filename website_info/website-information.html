About Heavy Iron Modding & The Community!

Who are we?

Heavy Iron Modding is a medium-large community hosted in a Discord server with the same name. It was established in May of 2018, and was fairly small but has only grown larger since.

History

Where did we start?

Heavy Iron Modding was formerly known as "BFBB Modding", and started out in a SubReddit titled "r/modthecrapoutofbfbb" on August 18th, 2015 and was a small community known for modding the 2003 hit videogame developed by Heavy Iron Studios titled "SpongeBob SquarePants: Battle for Bikini Bottom". Modding was only possible with QuickBMS and hex editing at the time. A breakthrough in BFBB modding occured in late 2018 when Discord user Igorseabra4 developed "HipHopTool" in C# capable of extracting Assets from HIP/HOP archives and replacing them without file size limits. Soon after he developed a 3-D level editor called "Industrial Park" that to this day is the level editor of choice for BFBB, TSSM, and many more HIP/HOP games. in June of 2022, Seil and half-owner Penney Marie of a separate Heavy Iron Modding community merged into one Dsicord community to mod/cover all of Heavy Iron Studios' games from Scooby NO1F, to Family Guy: BTTM.
